Our Mission and Purpose

Our team's mission is to create an essential spiritual experience while also providing luxury amenities. The eclectic fauna and flora, the constancy of the land and the everchanging sea will provide you a wondrous experience of California.

Memorable California

Theres is no better place to explore the California landscape than Big Sur. Here, you'll find ancient redwood forests, pristine coastal waters full of wildlife. You won't be surprised at the great care Californians have taken to preserve these vistas.

Wellgroomed Trails

We pride ourselves in the attention we give to our guests and the Zen atmosphere we help create. Our teams goal is to help our guests get in touch with the silent wonders of nature. Walk with us under the warm golden light of California.

California Scenery

Our woodlands and coastal sage are ancient and contain trails as old as California herself. Visitors have an almost automatic connection to the land here when they first explore our woodlands and coastal eco systems.
